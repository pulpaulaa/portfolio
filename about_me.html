Paula Santa María


Hey there! I'm Paula, a game designer, storyteller, and artist from Chile.

With a background in user-centered design, I approach each project with a mix of creativity and analytical thinking and enjoy bridging the gap between its different aspects. I like to contribute to projects that challenge the boundaries of game design and innovation, creating immersive gameplay experiences that resonate with players.

I believe that games can positively impact on people's lives, connecting them with their emotions and experiences, learning about themselves through someone else’s story. My goal is to participate in this kind of meaningful projects, making game design choices that create a fully emotional experience for the player, promoting mental health, interculturality and introspection.

In 2023, I was selected for the IGDA Foundation’s Virtual Exchange, a competitive mentorship program, being part of the International Scholars cohort, and later attended GDC 2024 with their support. More recently, my thesis project was nominated for the Best Student Game Awards at the Student Games Festival 2025 in Warsaw, Poland. I was also selected for Pro Helvetia’s 2026 She Got Game mentorship program, focused on female-empowering games.

When I'm not working, I'm usually reading, painting, watching TV shows, playing beautiful games, singing or spending time with my friends, family and cats.
