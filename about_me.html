Paula Santa María


Hey there! I'm Paula, a design student and game developer from Chile.

My journey has led me to explore a wide range of skills independently, including programming, illustration, animation, and writing. This continuous learning process as well as my degree in integral design have made me see projects in a holistic and interdisciplinary perspective. I enjoy making connections between all the different parts of a project.

I believe that games can positively impact on people's lives, connecting them with their emotions and experiences, learning about themselves through someone else’s story. My goal is to participate in this kind of meaningful projects, making game design choices that create a fully emotional and psychological experience for the player, promoting mental health and introspection. Some of my favorite indie games that achieve this are Spiritfarer, Celeste and Gris.

One the achievements I’m most proud of is that in 2023 I was selected as a recipient for the International Game Developers Association (IGDA) Foundation's Virtual Exchange program, being a part of the International Scholars cohort.

When I'm not working, I'm usually painting, reading fiction books, watching Netflix, playing beautiful games, singing or spending time with my friends, family and cats.
