Paula Santa María


Hey there! I'm Paula, a game designer, storyteller, and artist from Chile.

With a background in user-centered design, I approach each project with a mix of creativity and analytical thinking and enjoy bridging the gap between its different aspects. I like to contribute to projects that challenge the boundaries of game design and innovation, creating immersive gameplay experiences that resonate with players.

I believe that games can positively impact on people's lives, connecting them with their emotions and experiences, learning about themselves through someone else’s story. My goal is to participate in this kind of meaningful projects, making game design choices that create a fully emotional and psychological experience for the player, promoting mental health and introspection.

One of the achievements I’m proudest of is that in 2023 I was selected as a recipient for the International Game Developers Association (IGDA) Foundation's Virtual Exchange program, being a part of the International Scholars cohort; and then got the chance to attend GDC 2024 thanks to their support.

When I'm not working, I'm usually reading, painting, watching TV shows, playing beautiful games, singing or spending time with my friends, family and cats.
