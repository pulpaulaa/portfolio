Industrial Design


Lamp XVIII

An industrial-style lamp inspired by antique library lighting. Manufactured using wood and metal bending techniques, with mechanical, welded, and epoxy adhesive joints. The base is made of cement and low-density polystyrene.



Hexagonal Cat Tower

A modern, hexagonal, two-story cat tower designed for scratching, climbing, and resting. Made from 12mm plywood using CNC cutting techniques, it is assembled entirely without glue, relying on interlocking joints for stability.



Sun & Moon Lamp

This Japanese-inspired lamp is laser-cut from MDF and white acrylic. The design features two opposing panels: the sun side, with triangular cutouts for a stronger light, and the moon side, shaped with curves like ocean tides, giving a softer glow. The base and top are engraved with constellations and a compass.



Boa and Elephant Bench

A bench inspired by The Little Prince, resembling the image of a boa constrictor digesting an elephant. Made from 3mm plywood, it is shaped through wood bending and assembled using plastic cable ties. The bench accommodates two to three people and includes an IKEA-style assembly manual.
